A bit about me

I studied mechanical engineering at the University of Cambridge and MIT, specialising in product development and machine design. I have applied this professionally in design consultancy roles, previously with Alloy in San Francisco, currently with Kinneir Dufort in Bristol.

My professional interests include mechanical design, product development, and the application of the circular economy to real-world products. I am passionate about applying the skills I learned through my studies to a variety of projects and initiatives that strive to make a positive difference, be it through technology or advocacy. I am always on the lookout for new projects, interesting mechanisms, and new ways of doing things.

In my spare time I like to ride and race bicycles, go rock climbing, hiking, wander around museums, or play bike polo.

Please get in touch if you want to find out more about me, my work or my interests!
