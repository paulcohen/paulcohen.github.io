A bit about me

I am a third year undergraduate studying Mechanical Engineering at the University of Cambridge, and am currently studying at MIT for an exchange year.

My professional interests include mechanical design, product design, structural analysis, and sustainable energy. I am passionate about applying the skills I have learnt through my studies to a variety of projects and initiatives that strive to make a positive difference, be it through technology or through activism. I am always on the lookout for new projects, interesting mechanisms, and new ways of doing things.

In my spare time I like to ride and race with both the MIT and Cambridge University Cycling Clubs, go rock climbing, hiking, wander around museums, or play bike polo.

Please get in touch if you want to find out more about me, my work or my interests!
